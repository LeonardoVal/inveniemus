Inveniemus tester

Inveniemus (inveniemus) is a search and optimization library, focusing on metaheuristics. This library includes implementations of some toy problems for testing purposes (problems.testbeds) and as exemplars. Among the most simple ones there are: HelloWorld [link], sumOptimization(), NQueensPuzzle [link] and KnapsackProblem [link].

Metaheuristic is the base class of all optimization methods. By itself it performs a random search. Other methods are implemented in subclasses (metaheuristics), such as: BeamSearch [link], DifferentialEvolution [link], DistributionEstimation [link], EvolutionStrategy [link], GeneticAlgorithm [link], GradientDescent [link], HarmonySearch [link], HillClimbing [link], ParticleSwarm [link] and SimulatedAnnealing [link].

Open your browser Javascript console. In Windows use: Ctrl+Shift+J in Chrome, Ctrl+Shift+K in Firefox, F12 in Internet Explorer, Ctrl+Shift+I in Opera.
